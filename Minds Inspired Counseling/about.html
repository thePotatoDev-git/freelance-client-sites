Meet Your Counselor

About Stephanie Tuano, LPC, CRC

Stephanie Tuano is a Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC) in the state of New Jersey and a Certified Rehabilitation Counselor. Stephanie earned her Bachelor's of Science in Psychology and Psychiatric Rehabilitation from Kean University in 2013 and her Master's of Science in Rehabilitation Counseling from Rutgers University in 2016. As a counselor, she utilizes an integrative therapeutic approach by blending techniques from complementary counseling theories, to provide an individualized counseling experience tailored to each person's unique needs and goals.
