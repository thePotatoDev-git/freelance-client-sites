Meet Stephanie

Stephanie Santos, LPC, CRC

I am a Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC) by the state of New Jersey as well as a Certified Rehabilitation Counselor (CRC). I earned my Bachelor's of Science in Psychology and Psychiatric Rehabilitation from Kean University in 2013, and my Master's of Science in Rehabilitation Counseling from Rutgers University in 2016.

I hold five years of counseling experience working with children, adolescents, adults, groups, families, and persons living with anxiety, depression, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD), or substance use disorders.
