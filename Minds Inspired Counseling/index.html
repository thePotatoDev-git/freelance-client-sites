The Minds Inspired Mission

My mission is to provide the individuals I serve at Minds Inspired Counseling with a safe place to process their feelings and work through life challenges. My goal is to help individuals gain insight on how to improve their mental health by learning therapeutic skills to foster personal growth, independence, and healing.

We'll Work Together

At Minds Inspired Counseling, it is the goal of your counselor to assist you in this process towards growth, wellness, healing, and fulfillment through your participation in counseling services.

Assess

Gather information on your reasons for coming to counseling, as well as your past and current mental health history to properly assess your needs.

Identify

Together we will identify your needs and goals for counseling.

Develop

Develop a treatment plan with a selection of treatment modalities suited best for your goals.
