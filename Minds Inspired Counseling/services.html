Services

Counseling Services

At Minds Inspired Counseling, it is the goal of your counselor to assist you in this process towards growth, wellness, healing, and fulfillment through your participation in counseling services.

Minds Inspired Counseling offers counseling for:

  • Individuals
  • Children
  • Couples
  • Families

Sessions range from 45-60 minutes, on a weekly, biweekly, or monthly basis.

Your first appointment will be used to assess your needs by gathering information on your reasons for coming to counseling, as well as your past and current mental health history. After the assessment, you and your counselor will together identify your needs and goals for counseling and develop a treatment plan with a selection of treatment modalities.

Treatment Areas

  • Anger Management
  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • Gender Dysphoria
  • Family & Relationship Issues
  • Mood Disorders
  • Stress Management
  • Substance Abuse
  • Trauma
